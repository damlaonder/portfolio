about me

I am a Turkish-American student based in New York, NY. I study Computer Science at New York University. I started my collegiate education as a Liberal Arts/Economics major before switching in 2022. In school, I've been learning python, java, data structures, and more. To learn as much as I can, outside of class, I am teaching myself web development with HTML/CSS by practicing coding and designing websites.

My career goals as a computer scientist are centered around making books more accessible for children. I hope to use the skills I learn with my degree to one day build databases and websites that make it easier for kids and students to find books that will continue to inspire them to learn about the world and allow them to see themselves in the stories they read.

I am currently an intern at Start Lighthouse, a nonprofit that works to make books more accessible to kids in the Bronx. I'm gaining knowledge about the various tasks involved in operating a successful non-profit organization and understanding the efforts required to enhance children's access to books.
